OutKrop is the #1 rockin' online community for you to converge with your friends and meet new people! Here, you can find the profiles of characters from my headworld "The Rock Records". My two main stories are Nonconformity, a battle of the bands story, and Uranium Rush, a psychological horror that takes place in a dying desert town. Fun fact: All of my characters are based off of rocks and minerals.

Content warning: Crude language and humor, cartoon violence, mature themes, moving/flashing gifs

Featured character: TB Nicholson

TB Nicholson is a PhD dropout with the demeanor of an old west outlaw. After moving back to his hometown of Sandstone Springs, he has been nothing but a nuisance to the townsfolk living there. TB once wanted to achieve great things, but nowadays struggles to find any ambition, choosing rather to get stoned and creating something awful (possibly radioactive) in his makeshift lab (his garage) to pass the time. Yes, his name stands for "tuberculosis"!
