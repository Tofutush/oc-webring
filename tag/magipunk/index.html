Enter the Sevenfold

Main page | OC page

By ORION ☆ OVERDRIVE

Not mobile-friendly
Science fantasy
Magipunk

Forever and an age ago, seven worlds banded together to defeat a common foe. Now, in the middle of an age of peace, strife sinks its claws in the populations, new threats rear their ugly heads, and a familiar, ancient evil threatens the worlds once more. Will the worlds come together as before and face their perils as one, or will they each fall to their own folly?

Featured character: Keyshia Fields

A former captive of the Fae realm that now seeks to right her mistakes and make up for lost time.
