MECHABIOSIS follows the protagonist, the Rouge Project Star robot, a robot reject trying to find purpose under his brother's guidance while making sense of his own feelings of self-hatred.

Content warning: suicidal ideation mentioned

Featured character: Rogue Project Star

The Rogue Project Star robot, otherwise known as RPS struggles to live up to his older brother's insistent requests of finding a purpose for himself, struggling at most tasks given to him that other's find easy. At times he can only wonder if maybe this is not something he'll ever be capable of. If maybe his existence would find more worth as spare parts rather than any purpose society and his brother wants of him.
