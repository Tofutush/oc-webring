Suzu and Jack follows the tumultuous relationship between 10-year-old Suzu Fallon and his emotionally distant, alcoholic father, Jack. Showing what it's like to grow up neurodivergent in Appalachia.

Content warning: Rating: TV-14 through TV-MA. Suzu and Jack contains depictions of child abuse, domestic violence, neglect, alcoholism, bullying, abnormal childhood psychology, and verbal abuse. Viewer discretion is advised. (More warnings on-site.)

Featured character: Suzu Fallon

Suzu is a bright yet bratty and defiant nine-year-old boy with untreated ADHD, navigating a turbulent life in the lonely, culturally isolated mountains of Appalachia. Though docile and loving toward smaller creatures like insects, he has inherited a mean streak from his father, and struggles with impulsive acts of violence.
