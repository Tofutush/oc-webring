Gates of Heaven

Main page | OC page

By Callilux

Not mobile-friendly

Gates of Heaven is a post-post-apocalyptic space opera that transitions quickly into a science fantasy. It follows the stories of several severely troubled individuals in their ongoing – seemingly impossible – efforts to find some peace of mind, with the added complications of painful immortality, dealings with questionable gods, and something being fundamentally off with the unkind world they inhabit…

Content warning: Sensitive topics, violence, suicidal ideation, sexual assault, incest, pedophilic abuse
