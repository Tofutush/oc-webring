My page has a collection of TV head and human OCs for a world called TV Where My Head Should Be! Set in a world where a mysterious TV head species called Televi began appearing in the 2050s.

Content warning: Violence, death.

Featured character: Aster

A friendly TV head who lost his memories after falling into water. He woke up in an unfamiliar world with no memory of how or when he got a TV put on his head. Aster goes on a journey with his friend Ashley to recover his lost memories and discover who he used to be.
