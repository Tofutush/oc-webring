Within the secluded universe of Earth-256-B, humanity has (surprisingly) persisted, despite the odds, into the far-flung future of the late 2990s. However, not all is well. Ville-de-Nouvelle-Yorke has been experiencing a severe increase in superpowered crime and self-proclaimed superheroes supposed to protect the city only care if it increases their fame! A guy named Virgil Voltspun gets bitten by a spider as a preteen and gains superpowers, so he decides to step in. However, he gets a lot more than he bargained for.

Content warning: Extreme violence, sensitive topics, swearing, arthropods, etc. More are listed on the Index page.

Featured character: Virgil Voltspun

A grouchy thirty-something that got bitten by a spider when he was twelve, gained superpowers from it, and immediately decided it would be a FANTASTIC idea to save people and beat up criminals for a living! He could finally put his extreme hyperfixations surrounding supers in general to good use and he could be like his role models: the Avengers! Years later, he's emotionally constipated and regretting his life decisions trying to balance school, work, bills, being a vigilante, mending his nonexistent social life, the list goes on and on.
